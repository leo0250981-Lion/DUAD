ALGORITHM: Preparing a Surprise Party for a Friend or Family Member

1- Start

2- Prior planning

3- Define:
a. Date and time of the party
b. Party location
c. Guest list
d. Theme, food, and drinks
e. Music and decorations

4- Confirmation regarding the friend/family member

5- Condition:

If the person is at home → plan a surprise entrance or delay their arrival

Otherwise → ensure the person will arrive at the planned time

6- Preparing the venue

7- While the person has not arrived yet:
a. Decorate the place according to the theme
b. Set up food, drinks, and desserts
c. Prepare music and sound equipment
d. Check that everything is ready and safe for the guests

8- Guests

9- Guest arrival cycle:
While not all guests have arrived:
a. Provide guidance to guests (where to wait, what to do)
b. Offer snacks or drinks if they arrive early
c. Maintain communication to coordinate the surprise

10- Waiting for the guest of honor

11- Condition:

If the person has not arrived yet and guests are already present → talk, play games, listen to music, or finish last details

Otherwise → ensure everything is still ready for the surprise

12- Arrival of the guest of honor

13- Condition:

If the person arrives at the venue:
a. All guests shout "Surprise!"
b. Take photos and videos of the reaction
c. Present decorations and food
d. Give gifts if there are any

14- Party activities

15- While the party continues:
a. Listen to music and dance
b. Play games or planned activities
c. Serve food and beverages
d. Ensure all guests are comfortable and engaged

16- Ending the party

17- Condition:

If guests begin to leave or the party comes to an end →
a. Thank guests for coming
b. Help clean and remove decorations
c. Say goodbye to the guest of honor and ensure they are fine

18- End of the party

18- End
