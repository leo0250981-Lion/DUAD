ALGORITHM: Preparing for an Important Exam

1- Start

2- Initial preparation

3- Preliminary activities:
a. Gather notes, books, and exam materials
b. Choose a quiet place to study
c. Organize a study schedule
d. Have pencil, paper, computer, and other resources ready

4- Study

5- Main activities:

Read the material

Review notes and summaries

Practice exercises and problems

Create conceptual maps or diagrams if necessary

6- Comprehension evaluation

7- Condition:

If the material is understood → move on to practice more exercises and take mock tests

If not → identify specific questions and re-read/review that section

8- Study loop

9- While not feeling ready for the exam:
a. Review questions and seek clarification
b. Re-read topics that were not understood
c. Practice exercises repeatedly
d. Take short breaks to maintain focus
e. Self-evaluate by doing mini tests or summaries without looking at notes

10- Repeat this cycle until:

You feel confident with the material

You can correctly answer exercises and problems

11- Final preparation

12- Activities before the exam:
a. Sleep well the night before
b. Prepare everything needed (documents, pencils, calculator)
c. Eat a proper breakfast and stay hydrated
d. Arrive at the exam location with enough time

13- During the exam

14- Strategy:
a. Read all questions before answering
b. Answer the ones you know for sure first
c. Then attempt the more difficult ones
d. Review your answers if time allows

15- End
