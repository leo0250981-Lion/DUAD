Algorithm: Preparation for Your Favorite Fun Trip

Start

Check the date and time of the trip

If the date or time changes → update the plan.

Make a list of necessary items

Clothing according to the weather

Comfortable footwear

Documents, tickets, or passes

Snacks and water

Cash and payment methods

Pack the suitcase

Check each item on the list and place it in the suitcase

If something is missing → add it

Prepare transportation

Check transportation schedule (car, bus, etc.)

Confirm reservation if necessary

Prepare food/other pending tasks

Eat before traveling if needed

Inform family or travel companions about the plan

Review the final checklist

Documents, money, food, suitcase

If something is missing → correct it

Leave for the trip

Enjoy the trip

End
