1- Start

2- Upon waking up

3- The phone alarm rings at 8:05 a.m.
a. Turn off the alarm
b. Get out of bed

4- Otherwise (if the alarm does not ring or I wake up earlier):
a. Check the time
b. If it is late → get up immediately
c. If it is not late → rest 5 more minutes

5- Personal hygiene (morning)

6- Perform:
a. Brush teeth
b. Wash face
(No shower is taken in the morning, shower is mandatory in the afternoon)

7- Preparing for the day

8- Multiple condition based on weather:

If it is cold → wear warm clothes
If it is hot → wear light clothes
If it is raining → take an umbrella
Otherwise → wear normal clothing

9- Breakfast

10- Multiple condition based on availability and time:

If there is time and willingness to prepare breakfast → prepare it and eat calmly

If there is no time or breakfast is preferred to be bought → buy breakfast and eat while organizing backpack/bag or while driving (only if safe)

11- Work based on the day

12- Multiple condition depending on the day of the week:

Monday, Wednesday, Friday (work from home):
a. Check computer and support tools
b. Work shift: 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
c. If there are support tickets → work on the tickets
d. Otherwise → drive Uber
e. Take breaks when needed
f. Monday and Friday → if energy and time allow → play soccer

Tuesday and Thursday (work from the office):
a. Check electric car battery level
b. Leave home with enough time to arrive at the office by 9:30 a.m. (approx. 1 hour drive)
c. Option: Have a quick snack while driving (if safe)
d. Work shift: 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
e. If there are support tickets → work on them
f. Otherwise → dedicate time to the Lyfter program to improve development skills
g. Take breaks when needed

Workday Loop:
While the workday is not over (9:00 a.m.–6:00 p.m.):
a. Perform assigned tasks
b. Attend meetings
c. Resolve technical support tickets

13- Lunch

14- Multiple condition:

If there is food prepared by my wife from the previous day → eat that food

Otherwise → buy lunch

15- Return home (only Tuesday and Thursday)

16- Drive the electric car
17- While driving:
a. Listen to music or relax
b. Option: Have a quick snack (if safe)

18- Personal hygiene (afternoon)

19- Mandatory shower:

Always take a shower in the afternoon, after work or after the day’s activities, without exception

20- Afternoon and evening activities

21- Multiple condition according to energy level:

If I am not very tired →
a. Drive Uber until around midnight
b. Charge the electric car while doing Uber or afterward

If I am tired → rest, play soccer if there is energy (Mondays and Fridays), or do lighter activities

22- Dinner

23- Multiple condition:

If my wife cooked → eat what she prepared

Otherwise → buy an inexpensive dinner

27- If I still have energy:
a. Do a little organizing
b. Check social media or messages

28- Otherwise:
a. Go directly to sleep
